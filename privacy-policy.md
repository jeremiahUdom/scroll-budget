# Privacy Policy for Scroll Budget

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026

Scroll Budget ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps you track and manage time spent on apps installed on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Scroll Budget, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you sign up, we collect:

- Your email address
- A unique account identifier (managed via Firebase Authentication)
- Your password is never stored or seen by us directly. Authentication is handled securely by Firebase Authentication.

### 1.2 Installed Apps and Usage Data

To provide the core functionality of Scroll Budget, we collect:

- A list of applications installed on your device, so that you can choose which ones to track. This list is retrieved using Android's Usage Access permission and is used only to populate your app selection screen within the App.
- The names/package identifiers of the specific apps installed on your device that **you choose** to track.
- Total Foreground Time (milliseconds) for each app you choose to track, obtained via Android's Usage Access permission
- The daily time limit(s) you set within the App

We only track usage for apps you have explicitly selected. We do **not** collect the content of your activity within any app — no messages, posts, browsing history, files, or media. We only measure _how much time_ is spent in each tracked app, using data made available by the Android operating system through the Usage Access permission you explicitly grant.

### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect device identifiers, analytics, crash reports, location data, or any other technical information beyond what is described in Sections 1.1 and 1.2.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Calculate and display your app usage statistics and insights
- Let you set and track progress against daily usage limits
- Improve and maintain the App's functionality and reliability
- Respond to support requests

We do **not** use your data for advertising, and we do **not** sell your data to third parties.

---

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

- Usage data and account information are stored on our secure backend servers, using a PostgreSQL database hosted via Prisma Postgres.
- Authentication is handled by Firebase Email/Password Authentication Service, which has its own security practices.
- We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Scroll Budget uses the following third-party services, which may process limited data on our behalf:

- **Firebase Authentication (Email/Password)** — for account creation and login.
- **Prisma Postgres** — for hosting our backend database, which stores your account and usage data.
- **Render** — for hosting our backend server, which processes requests between the App and our database.

These providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

---

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

- **Access & correction:** You can view and update which apps you track and your daily limits at any time within the App. You may add or remove any installed app from tracking at any time.
- **Deletion:** You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at ujsprojects@gmail.com. We will delete your account data within 30 days of a verified request.
- **Usage Access permission:** You may revoke the Usage Access permission at any time via your device settings. Doing so will prevent the App from tracking your usage.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Scroll Budget is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

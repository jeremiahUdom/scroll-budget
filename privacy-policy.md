# Privacy Policy for Scroll Budget

**Last updated: July 10, 2026**

Jeremiah Udom (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the Scroll Budget mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App accesses and uses information when you use the App.

## Summary

Scroll Budget is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information on any external server. All data used by the App remains on your device.

## Data We Access

To provide its core functionality, the App accesses the following information locally on your device only:

### 1. List of Installed Applications

The App reads the list of applications installed on your device so you can select which apps you want to set a time budget for.

This information is never transmitted anywhere and is only used to display the app selection screen within Scroll Budget.

### 2. App Usage Data

With your explicit permission through Android’s Usage Access settings, the App reads how much time you spend in the apps you choose to track.

This data is used solely to:

- Display your app usage compared to your set budget
- Provide usage insights within the App
- Trigger local notifications when you approach or reach your budget

Your usage data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, shared with third parties, used for advertising, or used for analytics purposes.

### 3. Notifications

The App sends local, on-device notifications to alert you about your app usage relative to your budgets.

These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not use external push notification services.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

Scroll Budget does not collect, store, or transmit:

- Your name
- Your email address
- Your age or date of birth
- Your gender or demographic information
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Your photos, videos, or other media
- Your app usage data on any remote server
- Analytics or crash-reporting data

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Android Usage Access Permission (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) | Allows the App to read usage duration for apps you select so it can track usage against your budget. This permission is manually granted by you through Android system settings. |
| Notifications | Allows the App to send local notifications when you approach or exceed your usage budget. |

## Data Storage

All App data, including selected apps, budgets, and usage history, is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

If you uninstall the App or clear the App’s storage through your device settings, this locally stored data will be permanently deleted because it is not backed up to any external server.

## Data Security

Because Scroll Budget stores data locally on your device and does not transmit information to external servers, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device.

We recommend using appropriate security measures on your device, such as a screen lock and keeping your operating system updated.

## Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Scroll Budget does not require users to provide personal information, and all app data is processed locally on the user's device.

# Privacy Policy for Scroll Budget

**Last updated:** July 10, 2026

Jeremiah Udom ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Scroll Budget mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use the App.

## Summary

Scroll Budget is designed to work fully offline. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data on any server.** All data used by the App stays on your device.

## Data We Access

To provide its core functionality, the App accesses the following data **locally, on your device only**:

### 1. List of Installed Applications
The App reads the list of apps installed on your device so you can select which apps you want to set a time budget for. This list is never transmitted anywhere — it is only used to populate an in-app selection screen.

### 2. App Usage Data
With your explicit permission (granted via Android's Usage Access settings), the App reads how much time you spend in the apps you've chosen to track. This data is used solely to:
- Display your usage against your set budget on the in-app dashboard
- Trigger local notifications when you approach or reach your budget

This usage data **never leaves your device**. It is not uploaded to any server, shared with any third party, or used for advertising or analytics purposes.

### 3. Notifications
The App sends local, on-device notifications to alert you about your app usage relative to your budgets. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external notification service.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, and the App does not transmit:
- Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- Your location
- Your contacts
- Any usage data to a remote server
- Any analytics or crash-reporting data at this time

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Usage Access (`PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS`) | To read per-app usage duration for apps you select, in order to track it against your budget. Granted manually by you in system settings. |
| Notifications | To alert you locally when you approach or exceed a usage budget. |

## Data Storage

All data — your selected apps, budgets, and usage history — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. If you uninstall the App, this data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered, since it is never backed up to any external server.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children, as the App does not collect data from anyone.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
